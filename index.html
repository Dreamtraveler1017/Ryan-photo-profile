Ryan

I am a 20-year-old student studying at National Tsing Hua University and a passionate street photographer. My dream is to one day become the Sony Photographer of the Year.

The camera is another way for me to gaze at the world. I love every afternoon filled with light and shadow, and I have also come to enjoy the city streets graced by a gentle drizzle. Whenever I pick up my camera, I am the most confident person in the world!

My advice is simple: use this device more. Rather than staring at a screen to know everything happening in the world, using a camera to capture the people right in front of you might just be the correct way to use this machine. — MediaStorm
